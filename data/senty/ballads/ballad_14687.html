Broadside entitled 'Public Executions'

Transcription

Public Executions.

Names of all the persons who have Suffered in Glasgow since the
year 1765, with an account of their crimes, and the year and day
of the month they were Executed, and exhibits a melancholy
view of the final end of all who deviate from the paths of' rectitude.
